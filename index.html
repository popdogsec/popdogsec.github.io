About Me

A Little Insight Into Me

Who Am I

My name is Benjamin Schafer. I am a recent graduate of Saint Cloud State University where I majored in Cybersecurity. My passion is security in both the digital and physical realms. I am always looking to learn something new to stay up to date in this ever changing field. This website is a way to share my projects, education and experiences with the everyone in the Information Security community.



What I'm Doing

Networking

I enjoy following the prosumer networking scene, especially Ubiquiti and Mikrotik. I can model and simulate networks in Packet Tracer and Opnet/Riverbed.

Home Lab

I run a Home Lab that consists of various virtualized Linux and Windows hosts, game servers, web servers, and a FTP server running on a Ubiquiti Unifi network.

Threat Intelligence

I love learning about threats and the history in how they were succesful. In particular I have an interest in the DUAL_EC_DRBG backdoor and the HAMMERTOSS malware.

Python

I use the Python language to automate tasks and develop security tools.

Capture The Flag Challenges

I participate in CTF challenges on hackthebox to improve my pentesting skills.

Locksport

I participate in the Locksport community to learn more on physical security.
